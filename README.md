# billionaires-wealth-analysis

This dataset contains information on 475 of the world's wealthiest individuals, sourced from global billionaire rankings. Each record includes personal details (name, gender, birth date, country, city), wealth data (net worth in millions USD), industry/category, self-made status, and country-level macroeconomic indicators such as GDP, CPI, life expectancy, tax rates, and population.
Descriptive Statistics (Sheet2)
The analysis covers key statistical measures across all numeric variables. The average net worth among billionaires in the dataset is approximately $14.8 billion, with a median of $8.2 billion, reflecting a strong right skew driven by a small number of ultra-high-net-worth individuals (max: $211B – Bernard Arnault). The average age is around 71 years, with the youngest in the 30–40 age bracket and the oldest approaching 100.
Age Distribution (Pivot Table)
The age breakdown reveals that the 60–70 age group is the most represented (106 individuals), followed closely by the 70–80 bracket (104), indicating that most billionaires built their wealth over long careers. Only 4 individuals are aged 30–40.
Top 10 by Net Worth (Pivot Table)
The pivot table highlights the ten wealthiest individuals, led by Bernard Arnault ($211B), Elon Musk ($180B), and Jeff Bezos ($114B), with a combined net worth of approximately $1.17 trillion.
